About EGAZE

eGaze is India's first comprehensive property management portal with a specific focus on rural properties. We are the only service provider who provides end-to-end property management services, in an industry where most other vendors only provide specific services, or service only buildings, or only urban areas, etc.

Welcome to eGAZE. Property Monitoring Software. eGaze is India's first comprehensive property management solution with a particular focus on land in non- urban areas. Our core mission is to provide property management services to owners living away from their property and act as a owner delegate with respect to any regulatory and compliance obligation for the property.

With a core team of veteran real estate professionals, rural entrepreneurs and cloud solution delivery architects, the team eGaze has a cumulative experience of over 30 years in providing real estate and digital management solutions. Commencing with our core property monitoring solutions, we will soon be offering end-to-end property lifecycle management services including acquisition, monitoring, maintenance and disposal offerings.
